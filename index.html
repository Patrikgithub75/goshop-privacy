Privacy Policy for GoShop

GoShop respects your privacy.

Data Collection

GoShop collects only the data required for core app functionality:

  • Email address and user ID (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Shopping list items and preferences stored in Firebase Firestore

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Synchronize shopping lists between household members
  • Store purchase history and app settings

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Tracking

GoShop does not track users across apps or websites.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: support@goshop.app
